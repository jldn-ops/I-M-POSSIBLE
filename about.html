About Jamie DeNovo

Jamie DeNovo is an author, entrepreneur, human behavior researcher and humanitarian whose work is rooted in a lifetime of “IMPOSSIBLE to I.M.POSSIBLE” experiences. She writes and builds tools for people who have done everything they were told to do—and still find themselves facing situations that feel stacked against them.

Fascinated by how some people collapse under pressure while others quietly reinvent their lives, Jamie set out to understand what actually happens in the brain when you are told to “believe,” “be resilient,” or “think positive” in the middle of very real hardship.
