The I-M-POSSIBLE Book Series

The I-M-POSSIBLE books are written for people who are tired of being told to “believe harder” or “think positive” while they are living through very real hardship. Each book takes the core idea seriously: even when we can’t change what happened, we can still change what our brain expects next.

The series blends neuroscience, imagination and practical tools to help you move from IMPOSSIBLE to I.M.POSSIBLE, one situation at a time.

The Brain-Building Program

The I-M-POSSIBLE program takes the core ideas from the books and turns them into a step-by-step way of living. Instead of a one-time burst of motivation, it focuses on small, repeatable actions that gradually change what your brain predicts is possible.

  • Belief-to-Outcome Maps: Simple diagrams that show how different thoughts send your brain down very different paths.
  • Micro-Blueprints: Short, specific tools you can use in real situations when everything feels too much.
  • Write-In Practices: Guided pages that help you track what you tried, what happened, and what your brain is learning.
  • Real-Life Labs: Practical experiments you can run in your relationships, work and daily life to test new patterns safely.

The program can be used on your own through the books, or supported through I-M-POSSIBLE Coaching and Workshops.
