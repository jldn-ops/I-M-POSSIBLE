I-M-POSSIBLE® Book Series & Brain-Building Blueprint

The I-M-POSSIBLE® Book Series is created for people who have done everything they were told to do and still find themselves facing situations that feel impossible. The books introduce a micro-blueprint for building your brain, developing a new way of thinking, and reclaiming a measure of control over how you respond to what is happening—so you can influence the best possible outcome, even when life feels deeply unfair.

Instead of offering vague “believe to achieve” slogans, the I-M-POSSIBLE® series walks you through specific, brain-based steps for moving from IMPOSSIBLE beliefs to I.M.POSSIBLE beliefs in situations that matter most to you.

The I-M-POSSIBLE® Book Series – Class 16

Book 1 – I-M-POSSIBLE MUSCLE® for the Mind

Current cover text: “I.M.POSSIBLE MUSCLE for the MIND”

Write-In Exercises & Practice Labs

A core feature of the I-M-POSSIBLE® approach is its emphasis on writing and practice. Many sections of I-M-POSSIBLE MUSCLE® for the Mind and the companion write-in book are designed so that you are not just reading about change—you are physically mapping your own beliefs, patterns and experiments on paper.

The books guide you through:

  • Identifying where your brain currently predicts “IMPOSSIBLE.”
  • Designing I.M.POSSIBLE® tests—small, concrete actions you can take in the real world.
  • Recording what happens, what you notice, and how your brain responds over time.

This repeated loop of mapping, experimenting and reflecting is what gradually builds an I-M-POSSIBLE® Muscle in the mind.
