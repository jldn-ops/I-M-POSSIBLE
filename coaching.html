I-M-POSSIBLE® Coaching & Consulting

I-M-POSSIBLE® Coaching & Consulting is designed for people and organizations facing situations that feel deeply unfair, overwhelming or flat-out impossible. Instead of forcing you to “just think positive,” I-M-POSSIBLE® Coaching teaches you how to work with your brain as it is today, and then deliberately build new I-M-POSSIBLE® patterns over time.

Sessions draw on the same belief charts, micro-blueprints and write-in processes introduced in I-M-POSSIBLE MUSCLE® for the Mind, adapted to your real-world goals and constraints.

Educational Coaching – Class 41

Under Class 41, I-M-POSSIBLE® Coaching is offered as an educational, training-focused service that helps you learn and apply the I-M-POSSIBLE® framework to specific areas of your life or work.

  • I-M-POSSIBLE® mindset and performance coaching for individuals and teams.
  • Goal-specific brain-building plans and practice labs.
  • Guided use of I-M-POSSIBLE® belief charts and write-in exercises.
  • Support for applying I-M-POSSIBLE® tools to projects, careers and relationships.

The focus is on teaching practical skills and processes you can continue to use long after the coaching engagement ends.

Personal Support – Class 45 (Where Applicable)

Where consistent with the I-M-POSSIBLE® Class 45 registration, services may also include structured, non-clinical personal support for people navigating difficult life situations, setbacks or major transitions. These services use the I-M-POSSIBLE® framework to help you:

  • Rebuild a sense of possibility when life feels stuck or shattered.
  • Design small, realistic I-M-POSSIBLE® tests in areas that matter most to you.
  • Develop new patterns of response that support healing, stability and forward motion.
