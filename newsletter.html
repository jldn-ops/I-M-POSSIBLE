I-M-POSSIBLE® NEWSCLUB & Power Posts

The purpose of I-M-POSSIBLE® is to find new ways to help more people help themselves—starting from wherever they are now—and to maximize their opportunities for a happy, healthy, and productive life.

I-M-POSSIBLE® Power Posts are short, practical pieces designed to help you navigate real situations—discouragement, overload, betrayal, exhaustion, unfair systems—and make one grounded, brain-aware shift at a time.

The NEWSCLUB brings these posts together, along with previews from I-M-POSSIBLE® workbooks, posts, newsletters, and upcoming projects.

Recent I-M-POSSIBLE® Power Posts

  • When Your Brain Says “Why Bother?” — A practical way to work with discouragement instead of fighting it.
  • The Law of Subtraction — What to remove before your brain has room to notice anything new.
  • Tests, Not Proof — Small I-M-POSSIBLE® experiments that don’t require belief, only curiosity.
