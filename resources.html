Resources

This section gathers I-M-POSSIBLE®-related ideas, reflections and future article-style pieces that support the work in the books, NEWSCLUB, and coaching or workshops.

Some resources will expand on questions readers ask. Others will explore themes that keep showing up in real conversations: invisible work, long-term stress, discouragement that won’t respond to slogans, and the quiet forms of courage it takes to keep going.

  • When “Hope” Feels Dangerous

  • Invisible Work, Invisible Wounds
    Naming the unpaid, unseen effort your brain keeps score of, and how that affects belief in change.
  • I-M-POSSIBLE® in Families
    Micro-shifts that don’t require everyone to agree or change at once.
  • Living with Unfair Systems
    What you can still influence when you can’t “fix” the system itself.
