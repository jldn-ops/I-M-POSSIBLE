I-M-POSSIBLE® Speaking & Workshops

Jamie DeNovo brings the I-M-POSSIBLE® method to audiences through keynotes, interactive talks and workshops that blend neuroscience, story and practical tools. Her speaking is designed for real people facing real constraints—not for perfect conditions or fairy-tale endings.

Drawing from I-M-POSSIBLE MUSCLE® for the Mind, the belief charts and her collaboration with Dr. Bryan Kolb, Jamie helps audiences see how their brains are currently predicting defeat, and how to begin building an I-M-POSSIBLE® prediction pattern instead.

Sample I-M-POSSIBLE® Topics

  • How to Apply I-M-POSSIBLE® Thinking to Turbocharge Your Brain
    Why “impossible” is a brain prediction, not a final verdict—and how to change that prediction.
  • What to Do When “Believe to Achieve” Isn’t Working
    How to work with a discouraged brain that no longer trusts slogans or promises.
  • Why Think Positive Advice Can Make You Feel Worse
    The neuroscience of invalidated pain, and what to do instead.
  • When What Doesn’t Kill You Is Making You Weaker – Not Stronger
    Understanding overload, depletion and how to rebuild an I-M-POSSIBLE® capacity for change.
  • Why Thinking Outside the Box Could Be Keeping You In The Box
    Rethinking creativity and problem solving when the “box” is actually your brain’s safety system.

Formats & Audiences

I-M-POSSIBLE® keynotes and workshops can be tailored for:

  • Organizations under pressure or in transition.
  • Teams rebuilding after crisis, restructuring or conflict.
  • Community, faith-based or non-profit groups supporting people in hard seasons.
  • Events focused on resilience, mental well-being, performance or reinvention.

Sessions can range from short keynotes to half-day or full-day formats, including interactive practice segments using I-M-POSSIBLE® belief charts and micro-blueprints.
