I-M-POSSIBLE® Personal Support Services

I-M-POSSIBLE® Personal Support Services provide structured, non-clinical guidance for people navigating difficult life situations, setbacks or transitions. These services use the I-M-POSSIBLE® framework to help you hold on to what is real and begin to rebuild a sense of possibility.

The focus is not on pretending everything is fine. It is on finding small, realistic I-M-POSSIBLE® openings in situations where your brain has learned to expect more loss, disappointment or defeat.

Examples of Support Services – Class 45

  • I-M-POSSIBLE® Life Reinvention Support: For seasons when the old map no longer works and you are trying to design a different future from the middle of the wreckage.
  • I-M-POSSIBLE® Resilience & Recovery Support: For people rebuilding after betrayal, loss, burnout or long-term adversity.
  • Guided I-M-POSSIBLE® Practice Plans: Support in creating and walking through small I-M-POSSIBLE® tests in specific areas of your life.

These services are framed and delivered in alignment with the I-M-POSSIBLE® Class 45 registration.

Important Clarifications

I-M-POSSIBLE® Personal Support Services:

  • Do not replace medical, psychological or therapeutic treatment.
  • Do not offer diagnosis, prescription or clinical interventions.
  • Can be used alongside professional care where appropriate, focusing on practical, I-M-POSSIBLE® tools and perspectives.

If you are in crisis or need clinical support, please reach out to qualified medical or mental health professionals or local emergency services in your area.
